About Drestein

Drestein is the flagship technical fest of the Department of Mechanical Engineering, celebrating innovation, creativity, and engineering excellence. For over 15 years, we've brought together brilliant minds from across the nation to compete, collaborate, and create.

Join us for two days of technical competitions, workshops, guest lectures, and networking opportunities that will shape the future of mechanical engineering.
